Aug. 24 1922

Re-union was called to order by Pres. James M. Parks. Rev. Clifford Young offered prayer. Singing of Campbell Reunion Song1. Mabel Shipman Shaw presided at piano. Welcome Address given my Llewellyn Shaw. Readings of last minutes read by Secretary. Piano solo by Eloise Merritt.

Speech by C. H. Congdon. Talks by Emma Buck, M. B. Seely, Chas Blackwell, Rev. Clifford Young, Rev. John Miller.

B. J. Kemp was elected President for the following year. Helen Kemp was elected Secretary and treasurer. Motion made and carried that the Reunion be held the third Thursday in August hereafter but if such date conflicts with the Odd Fellows Picnic or Grange Picnic of Tioga Co, the President an change the date to the 4th Thursday.

Signed by Sec
Emma L. Parks
