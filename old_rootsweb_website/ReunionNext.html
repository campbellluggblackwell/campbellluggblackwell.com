2020 Reunion of Campbells, Lugg & Blackwells of Nelson, PA

Come and join our first "virtual reunion." Anyone with a phone can participate -- as can anyone with a computer or tablet with Internet access. Usually we gather in Nelson, PA, but the status of the pandemic will determine whether or not an in-person gathering occurs this year. Regardless, ZOOM will allow all of us an opportunity to participate from home.


So, come to our virtual reunion and:

  • Make new friends & greet old ones;
  • Reconnect with your roots;
  • Have an interesting time;
  • Participate & help shape our future.
    •

The reunion is designed for descendants of our Campbells, Luggs & Blackwells, but it's open to the public, especially to those with an interest in the region's history & families.

WHEN. Sat., Aug 1, 2020, via Zoom beginning at 1 pm EDT.

WHERE. At a www.zoom.com "meeting." You don't have to buy or subscribe to anything. Those who want to participate by "dumb" phones are all set. But those who want to participate via computer or smartphone will need to download ZOOM's app.

WHAT. Doing a reunion on-line is new to us, so we're feeling our way and working on what changes should be made to our usual meetings to make the best use of this opportunity. Stay tuned.  And send suggestions and offers of assistance to billthompson76 AT gmail.com.

HOW. Details about how to register and an invitation will appear here and on our "Campbells, Luggs & Blackwells of Nelson, PA" Facebook group.

Unfortunately we had to cancel the planed in-person gathering. Hopefully we can schedule one next year.
